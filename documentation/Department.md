# Department Resource

The Department resource manages departments and department membership within the Zoho Cliq organization.

## Operations

| Operation | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Create Department | Create a department in the organization |
| Update Department | Update a department |
| Delete Department | Delete a department |
| Get Department | Get detailed information for a department |
| List Departments | Get departments in the organization |
| Add Department Members | Add members to a department |
| Remove Department Members | Remove members from a department |
| Get Department Members | Get list of members in a department |
